About me

I received a bachelor's degree in computer science and technology from the Beijing University of Technology in 2016 and started to study for a master's degree at the Communication University of China in 2020. I am currently a master's student at the Media Big Data Center of the State Key Laboratory of Media Convergence and Communication, Communication University of China. My supervisor is Professor Hao Shen, a leader in the field of journalism and communication in China. In 2019, I worked as a scientific research intern at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences for one year, and participated in the research work on several topics under the guidance of Professor Haiming Chen. My main research interests include natural language processing, deep learning, and recommender systems. In addition, I have also done some research on knowledge graphs, such as the Chinese short text matching optimization algorithms based on external knowledge. I focus on using AI to solve real problems in journalism.
